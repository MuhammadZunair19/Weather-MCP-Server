# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want this project to be welcoming, respectful, and constructive for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and patient
- Assume good intent
- Give actionable technical feedback
- Focus criticism on ideas and implementation, not people

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Trolling, intimidation, or repeated hostility
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or limit participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.
